# The Alpha Sovereign: Risk & Sizing Protocol

This protocol defines the strict money-management laws of the Alpha Sovereign Engine. While the *Alpha Quad Matrix* and *Fourier Phase Reversal* govern **Selection** and **Timing**, this document governs **Survival** via **Sizing**.

If you bet 100% of the portfolio on a single "Score 100" asset, you violate the First Principle of Survival: Ergodicity. Even a company with perfect mathematical physics can be halted by an exogenous shock, fraud, or geopolitical force majeure. 

To ensure the engine can compound infinitely without risking a total wipeout, the following three inviolable mathematical limits are placed on execution:

## 1. The Concentration Limit (Max 20%)
*   **The Rule:** No single ticker may ever exceed a **20% allocation** of total portfolio equity, regardless of its Alpha Quad Score.
*   **The Rationale:** Even the most pristine "Super Cycle" (e.g., NVDA in 2024 or CSCO in 1999) carries idiosyncratic tail risk. Capping exposure at 20% ensures that an unforeseeable 50% catastrophic gap-down in a single asset only inflicts a 10% maximum drawdown on the Sovereign.
*   **Execution:** If a position organically grows beyond 20% due to price appreciation, it must be mechanically trimmed back to the 20% threshold, harvesting yield and reallocating to cash or the next highest-scoring asset.

## 2. The Correlation Cap (Max 60% Physics Cluster)
*   **The Rule:** No single "Physics Cluster" (e.g., Semiconductors, AI Datacenter Supply Chain, GLP-1 Pharmaceuticals) may exceed a **60% combined allocation**.
*   **The Rationale:** If NVDA, AMD, TSM, and MU all simultaneously score 100 in the Alpha Quad Matrix, they are fundamentally executing on the exact same underlying catalyst (AI CapEx). They possess roughly 85% covariance. Being 80% long a single cluster represents leveraged fragility, not diversification. 
*   **Execution:** If multiple assets in the same cluster score 100, you must rank them by their Fourier Acceleration divergence or their Expected Alpha and select only the top contenders to fit exactly within the 60% cap.

## 3. The Hard Drawdown Breaker (The "Unknown Unknowns" Circuit)
*   **The Rule:** If the Total Portfolio Drawdown exceeds **15% within a rolling 30-day period**, ALL positions are immediately and mechanically **halved (-50% exposure)**.
*   **The Rationale:** The Sovereign model calculates risk based on observable physics (Earnings) and liquidity (Bond Yields). However, markets can be broken by "Unknown Unknowns" (e.g., March 2020 Pandemic, September 2008 Lehman collapse). A 15% rapid drawdown indicates that the structural modeling of the market itself has fundamentally broken, and correlation has gone to 1. 
*   **Execution:** You do not wait for the Alpha Quad to catch up. The 15% drawdown threshold acts as a manual override, raising 50% cash instantly to defend the capital base until the volatility resets and the mathematical regime stabilizes.
